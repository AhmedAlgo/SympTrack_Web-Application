What's this about?

This is SympTrack, a program that captures and stores the every day data about a patient’s major symptoms and displays the data at a weekly, monthly, and yearly level. Symp+rack also pushes the data to a patient chart system or directly to the email of their doctor’s office in preparation for related appointments.
